## Three target task includes :

1. Writing Assignment Reports (Collaborate with AI – Centaur)

Success Definition:
I can produce a well-structured first draft in less than half the time it would normally take. The final report clearly reflects my own analysis and insights, requires only minor revisions before submission, and receives positive feedback for its clarity, organization, and accuracy.

2. Researching Machine Learning Topics (Delegate to AI with Review)

Success Definition:
Within 10–15 minutes, I receive a clear summary of the topic, explanations of key concepts in beginner-friendly language, and recommendations for the most relevant papers or resources. I can quickly decide what deserves deeper study and verify important technical details before relying on them.

3. Trading Forex (Collaborate with AI – Centaur)

Success Definition:
AI helps me quickly generate market analysis, identify potential trade ideas, and summarize economic or technical factors affecting the market. I critically review every suggestion and make all final trading decisions based on my own analysis and risk management strategy.
