About

Before ML, I checked work against plans. Now I check models against evidence.

Before transitioning into AI and machine learning, I worked in construction, where checking work against plans and specifications was part of my daily routine.

That habit of verifying before approving has stayed with me.

Today I apply the same mindset to machine learning: questioning a result before trusting it, rather than assuming a convincing output is necessarily a correct one.

Currently in FlyRank's Applied Search Intelligence internship, building toward a decision-support prototype for content review prioritization.
Lanre Odesanya — built plain, on purpose. No framework, no build step.
